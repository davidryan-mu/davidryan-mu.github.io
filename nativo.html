Nativo - the language learning app

Nativo focuses on giving you a comfortable platform to practice the verbal aspect of learning a language. Speaking a new language is regarded as one of the most difficult areas of language learning to gain experience in as it can be hard to find someone to practice with in your own time. This is the problem our app seeks to overcome.

In Action

With Nativo, you are matched with someone fluent in the language you want to learn who happens to want to learn the language you are fluent in. You two would then connect and chat via email. This creates a great learning atmosphere for all involved and an opportunity to meet new people and learn about different cultures.
